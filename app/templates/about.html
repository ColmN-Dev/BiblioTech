About BiblioTech

Welcome to BiblioTech! We are a simple library management system designed to help you organize and manage your book collection.

What is BiblioTech?

BiblioTech is a web-based application that allows users to create and maintain a digital library of their books. It provides features for adding, editing, and deleting book entries, as well as searching and filtering capabilities.

The inspiration for the name Biblio came from the Greek word "biblios" (book) and Tech which combines the old "library" concept with modern technology.
